We aim to get this to you within 2-3 weeks. We're selling on pre-order, so in the worst case scenario, it could take longer, but we'll stay in touch with you and get it to you as fast as possible. Some orders arrive within 10 business days or less.

What material are your shirts and how do I wash them? +

Composition: 100% Cotton
Care instructions: Wash in cold water, tumble dry, and iron if necessary
Closure type: Pull On
